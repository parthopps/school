Kolej Tuanku Ja'afar welcome applications throughout the year however, there is limited availability so we advise you to apply as early as you can. Primary school applicants are able to enter the school during any point in the school year and secondary school applicants can join in either August (Term 1) or January (Term 2) intake. Everything you need to know about how to apply is outlined below.

STEP 1 - APPLYING ONLINE

1Before applying check this documentto see which year group is correct for your child.

2You will need to make a non-refundable RM 350 registration fee. This can be paid by cash, cheque or bank transfer.

STEP 2 - FINALISING THE APPLICATION.

1Once your application has been submitted, you will be invited to the school for an assessment. Please note assessment is not required for Sixth Form applicants.

2You will be notified of the outcome of your application shortly afterwards via email. The offer will be valid for 14 days from receipt.

3To secure the offer of the place, you will need to make payment of the deposit and admittance fee.

STEP 3 - DECISION

1Once your application has been submitted, you will be invited to the school for an assessment. Please note assessment is not required for Sixth Form applicants.

2You will be notified of the outcome of your application shortly afterwards via email. The offer will be valid for 14 days from receipt.

3To secure the offer of the place, you will need to make payment of the deposit and admittance fee.
