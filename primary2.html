Home KTJ PRIMARY WELCOME FROM THE HEAD OF PRIMARY

At ​KTJ International Primary School we aim to provide your child with exciting educational experiences to engage and motivate them to reach their true potential

The Primary School is now five years old and our ethos and values revolve around challenging and nurturing every individual child - we want the children at KTJ Primary to flourish and enjoy their learning. We aim to develop lifelong learners who want to take on a challenge, who can

communicate effectively and problem solve. Our excellent teachers, teaching assistants and dedicated school staff provide a wide range of learning opportunities in a stimulating environment. We firmly believe in developing the whole child and our rich and holistic curriculum incorporates both the British National Curriculum and the International Primary Curriculum (IPC). Alongside subjects such as literacy, mathematics, science, history and geography our curriculum also has an emphasis on sport, languages, internationalism, music, drama, art and design. We recognise that supporting and encouraging the development of the children's character is an important role of the school and we have a focus on the IPC Personal Goals of resilience, respect, adaptability, thoughtfulness, morality, enquiry, cooperation and communication. At KTJ we know that relationships count and the school values of compassion, empathy and mutual respect are an important part of school life at KTJ Primary. We are educating the leaders of the future and there is no better start for this than at KTJ International Primary School.

The Primary School facilities are second to none and our students also benefit from access to the Secondary School’s wide range of resources. We provide specialist teachers for languages, sports and music who are involved in both secondary and primary teaching enabling us to meet the needs of all our children. In addition, this aids a smooth transition from Primary to Secondary.

Please do call and make an appointment to visit the school. We will be pleased to show you around!

JAMES TWIGG
