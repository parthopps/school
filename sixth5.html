Home KTJ SIXTH FORM CURRICULUM

At KTJ, we offer A Levels from Cambridge International Examinations and Edexcel. These give students access to universities throughout the world.

We pride ourselves on giving individual support to enable students to succeed, both within school and beyond, by ensuring they gain entrance to the university best suited to their needs. The curriculum is tailored to the students’ requests as much as possible, with almost every student taking their first choice in all four A Level subjects.

While you are building yourself outside the classroom, it is important to note that KTJ’s teachers are always ready to give you the academic support you need. Please do not hesitate to approach them for assistance or extra help. Given their undying commitment to your success, you should really get their advice. Our Sixth Form staff is also one that is very experienced in helping you adjust to the life of an all rounded Sixth Form student. They too tirelessly support Sixth Form events and give you more than sufficient help for your university applications. It is needless to say that you will find them to be your life savers most of the time. They are also a cool bunch to have a chat with!

Each student has a tutor who, in conjunction with subject staff and the Sixth Form team, will give advice concerning university choices and will work with their students to develop first class applications. To further support this process, we have visits from all of the top universities at least once a year, where students can talk to admissions tutors and have access to the most recent information. Whilst most of our students are bound for the UK, we know that many of our students will apply to more than one country and have developed good working relationships with universities at home in Malaysia, and those in the USA, Australia, Canada, New Zealand and Ireland.

The Sixth Form environment is focused on achieving and celebrating success in all areas and any student who wants to be part of this culture of success is very welcome here. Each year the school offers scholarships in its desire to attract top students. If we are offering what you are searching for, we look forward to meeting you in the future and supporting your own achievements.
