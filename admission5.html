WHEN TO ENROLL YOUR CHILD
KTJ Fee Structure 2019/2020
WHAT OUR FEES INCLUDE:

A child's maturity and English language proficiency will be considered, alongside their age. Typically, students that complete UPSR in Standard 6 will join Form 1 at KTJ. Those who have completed their SPM in Form 5 will join Lower Sixth Form.

In the Primary School, we expect children to follow the classes and ages as indicated by the KTJ class. We do not usually accept external students into Form 5 or the Upper Sixth as this is half-way through the IGCSE or A level course.
