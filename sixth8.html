SIXTH FORM CENTRE :

The Sixth Form Centre is a dedicated building for our Form Six students. There are educational and recreational rooms as well as a pantry.

The Sixth Form Centre allows students to work in a purpose-built environment with excellent facilities, where they also enjoy significant support from the Head and Deputy Head of Sixth Form, at least one of whom is available when the Centre is open. There is a Computer suite designated solely for the use of the Sixth Form, with free printing, and internet access. We believe that Independent study is important and provide two interview rooms that can be used for individual or small group study. The Centre also comprises a large and comfortable Common room with an attached kitchen and a games room with a table tennis and pool table, all of which are available to students in their free time. The Head of Sixth Form and the Deputy Heads also have offices here to provide individualised assistance to students on subject choices as well university applications.
