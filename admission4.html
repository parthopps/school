FEE STRUCTURE:
KTJ Fee Structure 2019/2020
WHAT OUR FEES INCLUDE:

The Primary and Secondary school fees include all of the textbooks and workbooks a student will need during their time with us.

PRIMARY SCHOOL:

For Primary School students, the fees include lunch and a majority of the extra-curricular activities offered. Only those extra-curricular activities that require an external coach will be charged to the parent. Additionally, any field trips that students attend are normally included in the school fees. Residential trips in years 3-6 are not included in the school fees and an additional charge is required. Nightly boarding fee of RM 200( years 5& 6 only).

SECONDARY SCHOOL:

For Secondary School boarding students, the fees include four meals a day; lunch, breakfast, dinner and tea. Laundry costs are also included in the fees and the student’s laundry is collected and returned daily. In addition, majority of the extra-curricular activities offered are also included in the fees. As with the Primary School, only those extra-curricular activities that require an external coach will be charged to parents.

CHARGEABLE ITEMS:

Parents may be asked to pay for certain school trips, with advance notice from the school. Additionally, there are a few extra-curricular clubs that may require us to hire an outside coach. For these clubs, the parents will be charged additionally per term

For boarding school students, there are some additional charges for house events. These costs amount from RM300 – RM1000 per term.

Any medical fees for students will also be charged to the parents.
