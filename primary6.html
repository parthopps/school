Home KTJ PRIMARY STUDENT LIFE

At KTJ primary we pride ourselves on our happy and cohesive study body. From the time that the gates open at 8 a.m. to going home time at 3.45 p.m., the children are given every opportunity to enjoy the facilities and space that are so fundamental to the nature of KTJ.

During the 20 minute break and one hour lunch, children are enthusiastically engaged with playing on the two sets of first class climbing frames, having fun in the sand pit, in the ride - on bikes area, in the painted games area in the amphitheatre as well as on the mini football pitch.

The core culture of the school is evident in how children of all ages, nationalities and first languages play together.

Students further get to bond with classmates in their weekly PE and weekly swimming lessons as well as music and library classes.
