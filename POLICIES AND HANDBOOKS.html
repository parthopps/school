CHILD PROTECTION POLICY

We are committed to ensuring all students enjoy a safe environment in which they can learn, develop and flourish. We recognise our moral and statutory responsibility to protect, safeguard and promote the welfare of all students. We are alert to the signs of abuse and neglect, and we follow our procedures to ensure that children receive effective support, protection and justice. We consider the welfare of each child to be paramount, and it is our stated intention to be proactive and diligent in ensuring every child in our community is kept safe from exploitation, abuse or harm.

Our Child Protection Policy provides the framework for achieving this. The principles and procedures contained in this policy apply to all staff and volunteers at the school. All staff and volunteers must read and commit to acting according to this policy as a condition of employment at KTJ. We operate an open and transparent environment where students and staff can report concerns without fear
