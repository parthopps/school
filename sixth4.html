Home KTJ SIXTH FORM WELCME FROM THE STUDENT

The KTJ Sixth Form, as we see it, is a very special place. It is a safe space for you to mature into a responsible, independent adult, yet a haven for you to seek help and support. The Sixth Form is one big family, caring for and helping one another in pursuit of academic and co-academic success, never forgetting to have fun along the way. We believe in integration and togetherness. You will find yourself participating in many social activities and making new life-long friends through the various events organised by the Sixth Form Committee.

In KTJ, as a senior, you would have the opportunity to become a better version of yourself by learning new skills and applying yourself in ways you might not have had the opportunity to. Students in the school run a plethora of different activities that you can try from Entrepreneurship clubs to Rock climbing. We even have an Anime club! Do not be afraid to step out of your comfort zones and experience new cultures as you might find your true self in some of these.

While you are building yourself outside the classroom, it is important to note that KTJ’s teachers are always ready to give you the academic support you need. Please do not hesitate to approach them for assistance or extra help. Given their undying commitment to your success, you should really get their advice. Our Sixth Form staff is also one that is very experienced in helping you adjust to the life of an all rounded Sixth Form student. They too tirelessly support Sixth Form events and give you more than sufficient help for your university applications. It is needless to say that you will find them to be your life savers most of the time. They are also a cool bunch to have a chat with!

As a Sixth Form student, school can be strenuous. The A-Level course might demand your hard work, university applications can be daunting, leading clubs and societies could be stressful. However, the familial nature of the Sixth Form between the students and staff, will always give you the reassurance and motivation to do well. We say it like they do in Hogwarts,”Help will always be given at KTJ to those who ask for it”.

Welcome to the family!

Jia Man and Aniq Hazis

President and Vice of the Sixth Form Committee
